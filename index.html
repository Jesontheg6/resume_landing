My Story

What makes me a great Product Manager

STARTUP EXPERIENCE

My experience with building a startup from concept to growth taught me to thrive in a resource-limited and deadline sensitive environment. I grew to work with the ambiguity and ever-changing direction of a fast growing startup.

TECHNICAL KNOW-HOW

I have personally developed several full-stack products and can streamline the prioritization process that values technical debt. Thus, I understand technical terms that allow me to communicate well with engineers. .

STRONG COMMUNICATOR

Having worked with a variety of people from delivery drivers to company executives, I have developed the written and verbal interpersonal skills to influence multiple stakeholders to achieve our KPI.

DATA DRIVEN

I have experience in deriving insights from data and whip it into a story. In making key product decisions, I have the background to analyze data hands-on in SQL and Python environment.

EMPATHATIC

I enjoy talking to users and understanding their problems. My outgoing personality allows me to communicate and articulate their pain points to better satisfy their needs.

CROSS FUNCTIONAL SKILLS

As the founder of HaloGas, my position pushed me to assume many different roles such as growth, business development, and engineering. I learned to develop a greater empathy for a diverse product team.
